Delicious Grilled Sandwich

Recipe


Spread condiments like mayo, ketchup, or mustard.
Layer meats, like ham, roast beef, or turkey on the bottom slice.
Add slices of cheese, like Cheddar,
Provolone, or Swiss. Top your sandwich with fresh veggies,
like lettuce, onion, and tomato.

Saucy Pasta

Recipe


Place a large pan on medium heat. Heat up cooking oil.
Add in sliced onions and stir-fry until fragrant and translucent.
Add in spices and bay leaves. Stir.
Add in stew base and allow to simmer covered for 3-5 minutes.
Add in maggi and salt. Taste and adjust for seasoning.
